About TranslationalAI

TranslationalAI develops clinician-led AI tools powered by LightProof SDK for verifiable, source-anchored AI outputs. Our clinical tools—Partner in Practice, LightScribe, and ChartPrepper—are deployed in production.

LightProof SDK enforces provenance during generation—every model statement is mapped to exact source text positions, and unverifiable spans are rejected. This prevents unverifiable output at generation time, unlike post-hoc validation tools.

All tools are aligned with PIPEDA privacy principles and provincial privacy requirements, with self-assessed alignment to HIPAA security protocols (not certified). They require no EMR integration and are already in daily use by clinicians.
