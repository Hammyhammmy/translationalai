LightEMR
In production · 2 pilot clinics
Intake Follow-up Clinical intelligence Kiosk Forms
A clinic operating layer that sits in the gaps the EMR leaves — intake, follow-up tracking, pre-visit summaries, check-in, forms.
Fax and email referrals are read by AI, triaged into a single prioritized queue, drafted into a reply to the referring doctor, and turned into an SMS booking link the patient taps to book themselves. Follow-up intent is extracted from signed notes, tracked, and flagged if it isn't done in time (90% precision against a 65-scenario test set). A day-sheet runs in the background — PSA trends, overdue scans, drug interactions, pending labs — and lands a one-line summary on the chart before the patient walks in. Multi-tenant; 2,680 automated tests passing; infrastructure cost under $100/month.
