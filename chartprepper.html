ChartPrepper

Referral summary tool deployed in production. Processes medical referrals to generate accurate, structured summaries that highlight critical clinical information while reducing cognitive load.

How It Works

ChartPrepper processes referral documentation through a secure interface, using AI to identify and extract key clinical details. The tool generates structured summaries with critical information highlighted, enabling efficient transfer to EMR or practice management systems.

  1. Input referral data through secure interface
  2. AI processes information, identifying key clinical details
  3. Receive structured summary with critical information highlighted
  4. Transfer summary directly to EMR or practice management system

Features

  • Structured summaries that highlight critical clinical information
  • Removes filler content to reduce cognitive load
  • Ensures no vital details are overlooked
  • Seamless integration with EMR and practice management systems

Security and Privacy

  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest
  • Aligned with PIPEDA privacy principles and provincial privacy requirements
  • Self-assessed alignment with HIPAA security protocols (not certified)
  • All data stored and processed securely in Canada
  • No data used for AI training or shared with third parties
