ChartPrepper

Referral summary tool deployed in production.
Processes medical referrals to generate structured summaries.

Accelerated Referral Review

ChartPrepper processes referral documentation through a secure interface, using AI to identify and extract key clinical details. The tool generates structured summaries with critical information highlighted, enabling efficient transfer to EMR or practice management systems.

Privacy-First Clinical Design

Secure dashboard with automated PHI redaction for referral documents.

Designed for high-stakes clinical environments, ChartPrepper automatically identifies and filters PHI while maintaining the integrity of clinical data. All processing occurs within secure, Canadian-hosted infrastructure aligned with PIPEDA principles.
