Our Clinic

We are an extension of the St. Joseph's Toronto Urology Department, dedicated to providing expedited assessment and specialized care for urological concerns. Our clinic focuses on rapid diagnostic pathways to minimize wait times and ensure timely access to expert urological care.

Our team of experienced urologists brings together expertise in prostate health, kidney conditions, urinary tract issues, and urological oncology. We are committed to providing patient-centered care with a focus on clear communication, evidence-based treatment, and streamlined pathways to diagnosis and treatment.
