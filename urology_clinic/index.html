Rapid Prostate Biopsy Pathway

Streamlined assessment for elevated PSA.

  • Rapid Biopsy Access: We prioritize booking for tissue sampling when indicated.
  • Transperineal Approach: Lower infection risk than traditional transrectal biopsy.
  • Biopsy Center of Excellence: We are a high-volume center dedicated to this advanced technique.

Gross Hematuria Workup

Urgent investigation for visible blood in urine.

  • Expedited Cystoscopy: Facilitated access to bladder visualization to rule out cancer.
  • Imaging Coordination: We coordinate appropriate upper tract imaging requisitions.
  • Expert Review: Direct management by Urological specialists.

Renal Mass Clinic

Expedited expert review for incidental kidney masses.

  • Risk Stratification: Expert review to determine if a mass requires intervention.
  • Surgical Planning: Direct access to surgical booking for malignant masses.

Ureteral Obstruction

Urgent management for kidney stones and colic.

  • Rapid Decompression: Stent placement for obstructing stones.
  • Stone Removal: Laser Ureteroscopy & PCNL pathways.

Retention & BPH

Management for urinary retention and catheter removal.

  • Trial of Void (TOV): Structured protocols to remove catheters safely.
  • BPH Management: Medication optimization and surgical options.
