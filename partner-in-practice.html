Partner in Practice

AI-powered clinical decision support tool created by a doctor for doctors. Delivers evidence-based insights tailored to each patient's unique needs and clinical context. Currently deployed in production and available for pilot use with select clinics and hospitals.

What It Does

Provides precise recommendations in seconds, ensuring decisions align with the latest medical guidelines. Rigorously refined across numerous fields of medicine, providing structured and comprehensive care instructions for a wide range of clinical scenarios.

Patient privacy is safeguarded - PIP does not collect or store any patient-identifying information (PHI).
