What we're doing

Make clinical AI checkable in seconds.

Most clinical AI today writes a fluent note and asks the doctor to re-read it to know what's missing or wrong. That's the part everyone complains about. It's also the part nobody has fixed by adding better prompts.

Our approach is to change what the model is allowed to write. If a sentence can't be pointed back to something in the source — the referral, the chart, the doctor's own seed note — the system doesn't produce it. Checking the output becomes a glance at anchors instead of a full re-read.

What we don't claim

Things we want to be honest about.

Anchoring a fact to its source doesn't make the source correct. A faithful summary of a vague referral is still a summary of a vague referral. The tool reduces what reading costs; it doesn't read for you.

Our audit is single-specialty internal QA — not a published clinical trial. We aren't pursuing HIPAA certification because we're built for Canada; PIPEDA and provincial privacy laws are what we align to. We'll keep saying all of this in plain language as long as it's true.
