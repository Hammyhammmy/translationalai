The Problem

Clinics receive dozens of faxes daily—lab results, imaging reports, consult requests, prescription renewals, insurance forms, appointment reminders. Someone has to read each one, decide what it is, assess urgency, and route it to the right person. That someone is usually a secretary spending hours on manual sorting, and the risk is that an urgent result gets buried under routine paperwork.

Fax Triage Dashboard automates the sorting. Upload a stack of faxes and AI classifies each one by document type and urgency, extracts key clinical details, and routes everything into prioritized tabs. Staff sees what needs attention now versus what can be filed later.

How It Works

Upload and Triage

Upload scanned PDFs—or have them arrive automatically from your fax line. AI reads each document, classifies it, extracts patient and clinical details, and drops it into the right tab in real time.

Prioritized Tabs

Faxes land in Urgent, Review, Admin, or Rx Renewals based on what they are and how time-sensitive they are. Abnormal labs and critical findings surface at the top. Appointment reminders and insurance forms go to Admin.

Clinical Detail Extraction

Lab results show extracted values with abnormal flags. Imaging reports show key findings and follow-up recommendations. Consult requests show the referring physician, reason, and attached documents. No need to open the PDF to know what it says.

One-Click Actions

Mark faxes as done, defer them for later, or email them to a saved contact—a specialist, a referring physician, or another clinic. Prescription renewals are grouped by patient for batch handling.

Built for the Whole Clinic

Multi-User Access

Admins get full control—upload, triage, email, manage contacts. Read-only users can view faxes and PDFs without risk of accidental changes. Access is granted through invite codes.

Customizable Routing

Default triage rules work out of the box, but admins can change which document types route to which tabs. If your clinic wants all imaging reports in Urgent instead of Review, one dropdown changes it.

PDF Verification

The original fax is always one click away. A side panel shows the PDF alongside the AI extraction so you can verify without switching windows. Keyboard shortcuts let you page through the stack quickly.

Email Collaboration

Save a clinic contact list and email triaged fax items directly to other providers. Sent items show delivery status and whether the recipient has viewed them.
