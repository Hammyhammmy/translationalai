LightScribe

AI-powered medical scribe deployed in production. Designed by a Canadian doctor. Generates accurate summaries, workup plans, and clean notes. Currently in pilot with select clinics and hospitals.

Features

Generate consult notes, SOAP notes, patient summaries, and custom templates. Simple interface with three buttons: Start, Finish, and Copy. Includes a text box for private inputs during patient interactions.

Security and Privacy

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Canadian data sovereignty - all data stored within Canada
  • Aligned with PIPEDA privacy principles and provincial privacy requirements
  • Self-assessed alignment with HIPAA security protocols (not certified)
  • Privacy by Design approach - automatically filters PHI
  • No data used for AI training

Status

LightScribe is currently deployed in real clinical environments, demonstrating the practical value of verifiable AI in healthcare workflows. Available for pilot use with select institutions.
