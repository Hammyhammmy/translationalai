Precision in Research

Biomedical research demands a level of specificity and accuracy that generic LLMs cannot guarantee. While standard models are prone to subtle inaccuracies and lack of grounding, our Data Science suite leverages the LightProof stack to ensure every data point extracted is anchored to its source.

Why Verifiability Matters

In clinical research, a "low hallucination rate" is not enough. To maintain scientific rigor, extracted data must be verifiable. LightProof ensures that:

  • Every extraction is uniquely tied to a specific line or section in the source document.
  • Automated validation schemas reject outputs that cannot be definitively proven.
  • Researchers can audit and trace findings back to the raw clinical text in seconds.
